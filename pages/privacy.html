TabStash Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 30, 2025

Data Collection

TabStash is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary for the extension to function:

  • Tab URLs and Titles: Stored locally in your browser to save and restore tabs
  • Favicon URLs: Stored locally to display tab icons
  • Timestamps: Used to group tabs into sessions

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API. We do not:

  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking
  • Collect personal information
  • Access data from other websites

Data Retention

Your saved tabs remain stored locally until you choose to delete them using the "Clear All Tabs" feature or uninstall the extension.

Third-Party Services

TabStash does not use any third-party services or APIs.
